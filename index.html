Our story

A love letter to Indian craft.

Saaj was born from a simple belief - that ethnic wear isn't just for weddings. It's for Tuesdays. For college. For chai with your best friend in the garden.

We curate handpicked sarees and oxidised jewellery that feel as natural on you as your mother tongue. Every piece at Saaj is chosen with care - rooted in tradition, worn with ease.

Come as you are. Leave draped in something that feels like you.
